Swim or Drown

Mission

My name is Gavin Tucker, I am the owner and founder of the Swim or Drown swimming foundation where we help kids learn how to swim. I did not learn how to swim until I was 13 and for any kid it can be hard to not know to swim. Our mission is to help kids learn how to swim and overcome their fears of the water. We will help every kid ranging from 1-11 to learn how to swim through optimistic and supportive teaching. We want to ever kid to learn how to swim and not give up on them
