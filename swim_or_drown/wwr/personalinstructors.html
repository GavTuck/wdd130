Intructor Miguel

Miguel Gonzales, is friendly and supportive instructor here at Swim or Drown. He has been an instructor for our company for around 10 years. He is a certified lifeguard with 5 years of experience and has a Certificated for Young Swimmers by the Swimmer Organization of America. He has helped thousand of kids to learn and no child is to difficult for him

Instructor Amelia

Amelia Brown, is a kind and loving instructor. She specializes with kids of younger ages and toddlers. She studied at BYU-Idaho university in Elementary Education. With her understanding of teaching kids she has specialized in helping them to learn how to swim. She is one of our best and has helped hundred of kids

Gold Fish Swimmers

The Goldfish Swimmers are for toddlers and younger learners. The age range is from 1-3 years old. These will be taught by Instructor Amelia and she specialized in teaching these young kids. These lesson are done once a week on Friday

Pufferfish Swimmers

Pufferfish Swimmers is meant for kids ranging from 3-8 years old. These lesson are shared between Instructor Miguel and Instructor Amelia. These lesson include more games and are much more interactive so that the kids are entertained and focused. These lesson are done on Mondays and Wednesday

Dolphine Swimmers

The Dolphin swimmers is meant for kids ranging from 8-12 years old. These group are lead by the instructor Miguel and these swim lesson will done on Tuesday and Thursday.
