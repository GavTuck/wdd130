Underwater Breathing

Breathing underwater requires specialized equipment like scuba gear or holding one's breath while free diving. Scuba diving involves using a tank of compressed air, allowing divers to breathe through a regulator. This equipment facilitates underwater exploration, providing a window into the mesmerizing world beneath the surface. Free divers, on the other hand, rely on breath-holding techniques, enhancing lung capacity and breath control
